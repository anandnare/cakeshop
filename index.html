About Timu

Founded with a deep love for rich aromatic coffee beans and decadent desserts, Timu has evolved into a local haven for foodies. We source organic coffee beans sustainably and bake everything from scratch every single morning. Quality, comfort, and community form our foundational recipe.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes! We have dedicated gluten-free cake alternatives and offer oat, almond, and soy milk options for all our coffees.

How many days in advance should I place custom cake orders?

We highly recommend placing custom orders at least 3 to 5 days in advance via our custom inquiry form.

Do you deliver orders?

We offer home delivery within a 10km radius for all orders above $20.
