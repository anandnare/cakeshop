About Timu

Founded with a deep love for rich, naturally churned ice creams and decadent desserts, Timu has evolved into a local haven for dessert lovers. We source fresh organic dairy and premium fruits sustainably, baking and churning everything from scratch. Quality, comfort, and community form our foundational recipe.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer vegan or dairy-free options?

Yes! We have a dedicated selection of fruit sorbets and plant-based ice creams churned with almond and coconut milk.

How do you prevent ice creams from melting during delivery?

All our delivery orders are packed in specialized thermal-insulated boxes with food-safe cooling packs to ensure they reach you perfectly frozen.

How many days in advance should I place custom cake or bulk orders?

We highly recommend placing custom orders at least 3 to 5 days in advance via our custom inquiry form.
